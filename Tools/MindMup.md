Web based application to create, share and publish simple mind maps.

## Detailed description & quick guide

MindMup is a mind-mapping tool that lets students create and share mind maps. Users can save or share their creations either on MindMupâ€™s cloud or through Google Drive. Maps can be customized with branches called nodes (known as parents, children, and siblings); nodes can be color-coded, and images can be added near a node. A storyboard feature allows users to look at their mind map from a different view, but it's best used once the map has already been created.
